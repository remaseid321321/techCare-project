Goal & Mission

Goal: The goal of TechCare is to provide a professional and easy to use platform that connects users with reliable technical services, including device repair, software support, and networking solutions, in an organized and efficient way.

Mission: To empower users by delivering high quality technical support that solves problems efficiently, improves productivity, and ensures the long-term reliability of their devices and systems.

Site Audience

Primary: University students and staff members who need daily technical support (device maintenance, software troubleshooting, network setup).

Secondary: Anyone with simple technical issues, plus learners/enthusiasts who can benefit from the Knowledge Base and guidance provided on the platform.
